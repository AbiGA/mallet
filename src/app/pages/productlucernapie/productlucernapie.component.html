Story

The Pie Chair takes inspiration quite literally from the mathematical figure ‘pi’, a rule of circles. The curves of the seat follow the radius of the same circle, making the design of the Pie Chair quite singular. As with all our pieces, form is married with function, and the low seating has been designed for optimal ergonomical comfort.

Design

This design was conceptualised from a simple line drawing, and was transformed into 3D. This allowed our concept to come to life, as we pushed the constraints of joinery and structural load transfer to create yet another masterpiece in the Lucerna collection.

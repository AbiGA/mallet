Story

The Robbie Sofa is a formal sectional sofa unit, with a solid wood structure and an ergonomically shaped solid wood backrest designed to provide superb back support for all body types. The grey upholstery offsets the warm tones of the wood itself, making the Robbie Sofa the epitome of grace and sophistication.

Design

The making of the Robbie Sofa involved the use of interesting joints to form the body and the structure of the sofa, as well as the uniquely angled stainless steel legs. The team also took great care in developing the backrest support system which is not only aesthetically pleasing in terms of form, but also functional and ergonomic.

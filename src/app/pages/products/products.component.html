The Wall
learn more arrow_right_alt

The Ratio Collection draws heavy inspiration from the classical orders of architecture devised in the Greek and Roman eras, which were first and foremost, the foundation for structure and construction, but also contained distinctive decorative elements. The name Ratio is a nod to the centrality of mathematics and proportion to the overall form, function and aesthetics of any design, as interpreted through architecture. By imbuing contemporary styles with a classical flourish, our masterpieces transcend time and space.

We tell a story with each piece that balances form and fantasy, solidity and sophistication, tradition with transformation. Take your eye for a journey over each curve, corner, edge and angle…

Lucerna

Lucerna highlights the beauty of the curve, reflected in the form and structure of each piece. Each piece has been inspired by the symmetry and harmony of the world around us - ranging from the mathematical formula for pi to ionic pillars in greek architecture as well as the weightlessness and brightness of Bali and tropical elements.

The Bratus Collection reflects a close connection to nature, and represents the beauty of nature’s contradictions: the rough edges of wood found in nature, with the precise, sleek measurements and forms created by man. The medley of these apparent opposites has created a singular collection that provides a small taste of the wild indoors.

Stump Collection

The Stump Collection is inspired by the symmetry of shapes and forms both imagined and discovered in nature. The strong lines and assertive presence of each of the pieces in this collection are what make it stand out, not only within Mallet’s repertoire, but also in the market.

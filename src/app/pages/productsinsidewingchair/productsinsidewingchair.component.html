Story

The foldable chair is a familiar yet timeless staple in India, and has taken many forms over the years. The Wing Chair draws on this heritage and plays with wood and metal for its structure, but uses woven cane to create the seating, a nod to its roots. The lightness in tone and material mirrors the other pieces of the Wing Collection, making it a chic addition to any room and a contemporary classic.

Design

The structure was created to mirror that of a traditional folding chair, with cane being an important reference to the original design. The chair folds with the help of a pivot and roller attached to the metal rib that slides in the channel grooved into the seat.

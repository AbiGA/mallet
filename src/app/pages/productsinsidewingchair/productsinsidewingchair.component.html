Wing Chair

Material: Oak, Caned Seating

Single seater

Story

This formal study table unit is inspired by the clean, aerodynamic lines of modern aviation, and works to create the illusion of weightlessness and partial suspension in mid air. The sharp angles and emphasized chamfers lend a dynamic and adventurous feeling to the previously solid and static interpretations of the table.

Design

Conceptually and technically, creating the Wing 1 table pushed our creativity and ingenuity to the maximum. Our craftsmen laboured to perfect each of the complicated angles and sharp lines to lend stability to the structure, while maintaining the illusion of levitation in mid-air.

Story

The Robbie Chair draws from the design language of the Bratus bench for the back rest, with a prominent angle to the back showing off its slender curved stainless steel legs that cross under it. It is the perfect complement to the Bratus, echoing its ergonomic design for maximum comfort and style.

Design

We created the seat from white oak with a cylindrical backrest, supported by a stainless steel structure, which is the main structural element. The stainless steel structure was crossed and bent in circular sections that clim to form the backrest.

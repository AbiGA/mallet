Story

Inspired by the art of Pier Mondrian, the Air Shelf is an assemblage of cubes and cuboids in a balanced and rhythmic manner. The partly open and partly covered shelves heighten the illusion of openness and airy feel. Functionally, the open shelves allow access from both sides, while also allowing for hidden spaces to store objects out of sight. The Air Shelf is also available with a low unit with three drawers for additional storage space.

Design

The main challenge with this piece was translating the abstract artwork of Pier Mondrian from the early 20th century to a contemporary 3-dimensional furniture piece fashioned out of wood. Much thought was devoted to the exact arrangement of the various elements, to achieve the modular yet perfectly unified aesthetic of Mondrianâ€™s work. In terms of craftsmanship, the process involved creating separate panels and adding in fixture details to complete the overall form and aesthetic of the shelf.

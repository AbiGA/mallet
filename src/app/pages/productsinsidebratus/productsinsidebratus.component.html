Pie Sectional Seating

Material: Beech, stainless steel and cushioned backrest

Catgeory: Casual Seating Area; Dining Room; Chairs and Benches

Story

Solid live edge 4-seater with a cushioned mid-back support, ergonomically designed for maximum comfort, yet retaining a rustic and nature-hewn aesthetic. The Bratus bench is the perfect statement piece that draws attention yet blends with any environment by lending a natural touch.


Design

In building the Bratus, we provided a slight angle in the seating to the back, to allow for a comfortable seating posture on the hardwood surface. While most wooden benches tend to veer on the uncomfortable side with lack of back support, the Bratus has a cushioned backrest designed to support the mid-back and as well as an elbow rest. We used stainless steel to create the legs, that provide superb structural support.

Story

This is a striking and luxurious table in any seating area. It is the perfect platform to showcase knick-knacks, or a statement sculptural piece, but can also hold its own as the centre of attention completely unadorned in and of itself. The smooth cement stained oak table top gives off a luxurious sheen, and the live edge on one side makes it the perfect pairing with the Bratus Bench. As with the Bratus, the tabletop is supported by stainless steel legs. This heavy set table is versatile, and its width lends itself to be used not just as a coffee table for multiple other purposes.

Design

In building the Bratus, we provided a slight angle in the seating to the back, to allow for a comfortable seating posture on the hardwood surface. While most wooden benches tend to veer on the uncomfortable side with lack of back support, the Bratus has a cushioned backrest designed to support the mid-back and as well as an elbow rest. We used stainless steel to create the legs, that provide superb structural support.

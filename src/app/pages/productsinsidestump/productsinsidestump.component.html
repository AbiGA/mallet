The Stump Table

Material: Cemented white oak

Casual Seating Area; Tables and Stands

Story

The masterpiece of the collection, it comprises four solid columns that resemble beautifully layered ancient tree trunks that form the base for a perfectly rectangular flat table top. The complex patterns and grains of each column is balanced by the simple lines of the tabletop and overall shape of the table. This unorthodox piece will definitely be a talking point in any shared space, with its solidity and assertiveness.

Design

The Stump Table represents our interpretation of the organic form of a tree stump. A parametric digitally created shape forms the base of the unit, topped by a pure rectangular flat table surface.

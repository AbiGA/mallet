Story

The Rick-Rack is a mid-sized shelving unit, which is designed along the same principles as the Wing 2 and Wing 2 tables. The highlight is the illusion of an imbalanced twist in the centre of the structure, that creates shelves of varying depths, with the solid wood border emphasising the illusion. This contemporary shelving unit creates a sense of fun and interest.

Design

Creating the Rick-Rack was a challenge in particular because of its unique form. The twist in each piece is manually created to match every corner of the shelf and is the product of a perfect union of design, craftsmanship and skill.

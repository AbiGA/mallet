Story

The Ergos is a standing chair, that envisions comfort and physical support in modern workspaces, but with a design that harks back to classical architecture. The solid curved form is a clear reference to the elements of classical Greco-Roman architecture, creating a sleek yet stand-out piece in any work setting.

Design

As is evident in its name, the design of the standing chair is guided primarily by ergonomics, and the comfort and safety of its users. The curved form was created to ensure maximum support and comfort, while being a sturdy and aesthetically pleasing piece.

Story

The Ionic order is characterised by swooping curves and slender forms, which are considered to be inspired by femininity, compared to the more masculine forms in the other orders. The Ionic chair is an ode to the curvature and balance in the Ionic order, forming an essential and fitting complement to the Ratio collection.

Design

A significant portion of the design and craftsmanship in this piece was spent on reinterpreting and marrying the curves and flat surfaces of Ionic architecture to the dimensions and structure of seating. We chose to mirror this in the swooping curve of the leg of the chair, while maintaining overall balance and functionality with the remaining structure.

Story

This elegant and compact stool can never be out of place! It is formed by a geometrically perfect cylinder topped with a woven cane seat, carrying the simplicity that is the hallmark of the Mensa collection. The muted color palette and sleek compact design allows it to blend into any setting, offering convenience and comfort.

Design

The cylindrical base was created using curved wooden slats in a brick pattern, that forms a subtle motif. The woven cane for the seat is a nod to traditional Indian furniture, blended perfectly with the minimalist clean design language of The Mallet workshop.

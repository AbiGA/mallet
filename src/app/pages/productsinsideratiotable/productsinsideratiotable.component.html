Story

The Ratio table is the centrepiece of the collection, with its sleek yet solid self-supporting structure and curves, referenced in the other pieces in this collection. The Ratio table is definitely a show-stopper in any setting, while also being functional as a working desk.

Design

The design involved calibrating and arriving at the perfect proportions which eliminate the need for a support, thereby making it a self-supporting desk.

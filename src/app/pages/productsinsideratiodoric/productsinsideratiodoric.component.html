Story

The Doric order is the earliest of all the classical orders, and stands for stark simplicity and minimal adornment. It is believed to be representative of masculinity, and as such, plays off the feminine aura of the Ionic order. These characteristics were the main inspiration for the design of this piece, with the legs and the back of the chair reflecting the smooth flutes of a classic Doric column.

Design

The design of this piece is more stark and sleek in comparison with the other pieces in the Ratio collection, with the smooth columns of the legs of the chair being the strongest reference to our Doric inspiration.

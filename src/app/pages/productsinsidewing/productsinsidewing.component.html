Pie Sectional Seating

Material: Beech

Catgeory: Casual Seating area; Sofa

Story

The crown jewel of the Lucerna collection. Low seating in an ergonomic design, the form of which draws inspiration from the concept of "pi" in mathematics, a rule of circles. The form reflects the concept of pi in many ways - every curve in the product follows the radius of the same circle. Its unique shape sets it apart from the run-of-the-mill seating options, a testament to the unorthodox craftsmanship that was involved in creating it.


Design

This design originated from a single line drawing. We then fleshed out the form in 3D, working out the specifics of structural load transfer and the constraints of joinery. This piece also reflects smart use of jigs and templates to create curves and tangents, and effective use of machines; overall, the Pie Sectional is not only beautiful, but also an effective and productive piece of furniture.

Story

Laid back is another term for this chair and you could see that Startis speaks for itself. The name Stratis means layers in Latin, in this context it reflects the crossings binding the two curves of the wood together. Spending some time on this easy chair would definitely be worth the experience, leaving you relaxed and peaceful.

Design

Stratis is very distinctive in its design with the wood bending at perfect proportions making it look very fluid. The lounger is made to look fluidic by fusing the back, seat and legs in wood. Whereas the hind leg and hand rest are made of metal.

Story

The Trigono planter stand was created through a seamless blending of geometric shapes with joinery techniques to create a two-level stand. The highlight is the juxtaposition of a complex triangular surface created through joinery in the lower level, contrasted with the perfect circle surface at the top level, emphasising on the stability and strength of the form. It is a multi-purpose complementary piece to the Bratus collection.

Design

We used a thorough and precise geometrical calculation to create the piece, using carefully calculated angles and dimensions, resulting in the beautiful harmony of the form and solid stability of the structure.

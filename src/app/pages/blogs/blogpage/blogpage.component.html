SOFTWOOD

Trees are generally classified into either Hardwoods or Softwoods. Inexplicably, not all softwoods are soft and not all hardwoods are hard. For example, balsa, a hardwood, produces one of the softest woods in the world. Conversely, Douglas fir, a softwood, is harder and has better strength properties than many hardwoods.

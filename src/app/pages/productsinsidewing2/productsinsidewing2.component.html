Story

A more minimal iteration of the Wing 1 study table. Also inspired by aerodynamics and aviation, this table comprises only two panels and one defined acute angle, completely embracing minimalism. The chamfers that follow the lines of the form enhance the clean and weightless aesthetic of the table.

Design

As with the Wing 1 table, conceptualising and designing the Wing 2 table was a matter of editing out the unnecessary details and refining the form to only the absolute essence. The single angle was cut by machine with the use of jigs and templates. We then added a wall mounting detail to support the desk.

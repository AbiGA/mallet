Story

Kagami is could be the piece you were always looking out for. Unlike the usual dressers, Kagami displays a style which is elegant in form and functional in design. Kagami is the Japanese term for mirror and so is the design typology.

Design

Kagami has a storage unit which rests on the crossings of the frames on either side making it look like it is afloat. One of the major attractions of the design is the slender mirror which provides enough space on the surface area for you to keep your daily necessities.

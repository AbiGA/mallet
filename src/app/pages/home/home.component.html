We are Mallet

Bespoke furniture hewn from nature by master craftsmen to create pieces that speak the language of international contemporary design. Each carefully curated form is imbued with sophistication, stability and harmony. We meld together artistry and innovation by reimagining traditional techniques for the new-age connoisseur.

Mallet stands for responsible luxury. We believe in maintaining balance and unity in all things, including our design - not just in our finished product, but in our business ecosystem. We source materials responsibly, and believe firmly in respecting our work, our people, our customers and the environment.


Our Story

Mallet reflects the coming together of traditional woodworking craftsmanship with a modern sensibility and global design aesthetic. We seek to create unique, custom furniture for the Indian market, with an underlying grain of innovative design and impeccable skill. If you are looking for luxury decor combined with sustainability, and international design with quality local materials treated with care and skill, Mallet is for you.

Story

A beech wood table top with an exaggerated grain pattern, tapered oak wood legs and developed truncated cone metal bracket. The Pick table is an integral part of the Lucerna Collection, with its aesthetically pleasing minimal design, and simple but effective structural solutions in a geometrically sound form.

Design

We achieved the geometry of the tabletop with set proportions and radius in a calculated ratio, such that every curve complements each other. The process of creating this piece involved the use of innovative jigs to taper the legs and applied geometry to achieve the perfect proportions with the metal brackets.
